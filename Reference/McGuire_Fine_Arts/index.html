Serving Frederick, Maryland for 15 Years

Welcome to McGuire Fine Arts Gallery and Framing. Located in the heart of downtown Frederick, Maryland, McGuire Fine Arts has been serving the fine arts community for 15 years. The gallery hosts a range of events including gallery exhibitions featuring internationally acclaimed artists. We also specialize in archival custom framing and unique gift items.

Gallery News and Upcoming Events

Remember Peace, Love, and
Rock 'n' Roll?

A 200-piece collection of new rock ‘n’ roll art from the 1965-1970 San Francisco music scene will be featured at the Gallery beginning on October 6. This art includes psychedelic postcards, posters, tickets and handbills from shows by legendary artists such as Jefferson Airplane, the Grateful Dead, Quicksilver Messenger Service, Chuck Berry, Ike and Tina Turner and many others, when they were just building their musical careers.
